# Privacy Policy — Zeiterfassung

**Last updated:** 1 June 2026 (app version 1.0.10)

This Privacy Policy applies to the Android app **Zeiterfassung** (a working-time
tracker, package name `com.macstacker.zeiterfassung`).

---

## 2 — Principle: all data stays on your device

The app stores **all** data exclusively **locally on your device** in an
on-device database. There is:

- **no** transmission to any server or cloud,
- **no** user accounts and no sign-in,
- **no** tracking, analytics or advertising,
- **no** sharing with third parties,
- **no** Internet permission in the published app.

The developer has **no access** to your data at any time.

---

## 3 — What data does the app process (locally)?

Only the data you enter yourself or that results from your usage, e.g.:

- recorded working times (clock-in/out times, segments, categories, notes),
- accounts and entries (working-time account, extra accounts, corrections),
- your settings (break rules, core time, standard hours, maximum limit,
  notification thresholds, language).

This data is stored **locally only** and used solely to display and calculate
your working time within the app.

---

## 4 — Permissions and how they are used

- **NFC** (`android.permission.NFC`): optional. Used only if you enable NFC-tag
  clocking, to clock in/out via a chip. If not enabled, NFC is not used. No NFC
  data beyond the clocking event is transmitted or stored.
- **Notifications** (`android.permission.POST_NOTIFICATIONS`): to remind you
  locally on your device (e.g. "standard hours reached", "approaching maximum
  limit", account thresholds). Notifications are scheduled and triggered solely
  on the device.
- **Boot completed** (`android.permission.RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED`): so scheduled
  notifications can be re-scheduled after the device restarts.

---

## 5 — Backup, export and sharing

- **Automatic backup** runs purely locally on the device (a backup when the app
  is opened, with rotation of the latest backups). This data does not leave the
  device.
- **Export full backup**, **share settings** and **time-data export** (PDF/text)
  happen **only on your explicit action**. Only when you export a file or share
  via the Android share dialog does data leave the device — exactly to wherever
  **you** send it (an app, location or recipient of your choice). The app itself
  sends nothing and does not decide this for you.

You are responsible for the safe storage of files you export yourself.

---

## 6 — Retention and deletion

Data remains on your device until you delete it in the app or uninstall the app.
On uninstall, the data stored by the app is removed by the operating system. You
may need to delete self-exported backup files separately.

---

## 7 — Your rights (GDPR)

Because no data is transmitted to the developer or to third parties and no
processing takes place outside your device, you retain full control at all times:
you can view, edit, export and delete your data in the app. To the extent you
have rights against the controller under Art. 15–21 GDPR (access, rectification,
erasure, restriction, objection, portability), you may contact the address above.
You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a data-protection supervisory
authority.
